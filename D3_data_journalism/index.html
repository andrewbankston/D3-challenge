Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Income!!

The graph above shows, state-by-state, the relationship between the percent of people who lack healthcare and the percent of people living in poverty.

Looking at the states with either the highest percentage of people in poverty or the highest percentage of people without healthcare, it is clear that many of these states are in the South.

While the correlation between poverty and lack of healthcare is not 1:1, it seems that as there is an association between poverty and lack of healthcare. Further analysis may reveal other factors and relationships. Uncovering these relationships is the first step to correcting this situation.
